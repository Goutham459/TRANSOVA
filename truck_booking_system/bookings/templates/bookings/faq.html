Booking

How do I book a truck?
Booking a truck is simple! Just follow these steps:
1. Register an account or login
2. Go to "Book a Truck" from your dashboard
3. Enter your pickup and drop locations
4. Select your preferred truck type
5. Choose your booking date
6. Complete the payment
Can I modify or cancel my booking?
Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking from your dashboard. Please note that cancellation charges may apply depending on how close the booking date is. Contact support for assistance.
What information do I need to provide?
You'll need to provide:
- Pickup and drop locations
- Preferred booking date
- Load type and weight
- Contact information
- Any special requirements

Payment

What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers. All payments are secured with encryption.
Is there a deposit required?
Yes, a small deposit is required to confirm your booking. The remaining balance can be paid before or at the time of delivery.
How is the price calculated?
Prices are calculated based on distance, truck type, load type, and current fuel rates. You'll see the complete price breakdown before confirming your booking.

Delivery

Can I track my delivery in real-time?
Yes! All bookings include real-time tracking. You can monitor your truck's location from pickup to delivery through your dashboard.
What if my delivery is delayed?
We strive to deliver on time. In case of delays due to traffic or unforeseen circumstances, you'll be notified immediately. Contact support for urgent issues.
Are the drivers verified?
Yes, all our drivers undergo strict verification including background checks, license validation, and professional training before joining our platform.

Account

How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a link to reset your password.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to your Dashboard and click on "Profile" to update your personal information, contact details, and preferences.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help you with any queries.
