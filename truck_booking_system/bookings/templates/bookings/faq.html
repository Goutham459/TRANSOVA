Booking

How do I book a truck?
1. Login to your TRANSOVA dashboard
2. Click "Book a Truck"
3. Enter pickup/drop locations (GPS auto-detects)
4. Select truck type & load weight
5. Review price & pay securely
Can I modify or cancel my booking?
Yes! From your dashboard: - Edit: Click booking > "Edit Details" - Cancel: Before driver assignment (24hr notice) No fee if >48hrs; 25% fee within 48hrs. Email support@transova.com
What information do I need to provide?
Required: - Pickup/drop addresses (or GPS pins) - Date & time window - Load type/weight (from Price Calculator) - Your contact number Optional: Special instructions for driver

Payment

What payment methods are accepted?
Cards (Visa/Mastercard), UPI, Wallet balance, Bank transfer. Secure 256-bit encryption. See payment success/cancel pages for receipts.
Is there a deposit required?
Yes, 20% advance deposit. Balance paid on delivery proof upload. Driver confirms receipt with photo/signature via ProofOfDelivery.
How is the price calculated?
Distance x base rate + 5% commission + load factor. Use /price-calculator/ for instant quotes. Transparent breakdown shown.

Delivery

Can I track my delivery in real-time?
Yes! Live GPS tracking on dashboard > My Bookings. Driver location updates every 5min. Notifications for arrival/departure.
What if my delivery is delayed?
SMS/email notifications for delays >15min. Track live on dashboard. Driver must notify before pickup. support@transova.com for disputes.
Are the drivers verified?
Yes: Background check, DL verification, truck inspection, 2+ yrs experience req. Company-vetted fleet only. View driver profile before booking.

Account

How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a link to reset your password.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to your Dashboard and click on "Profile" to update your personal information, contact details, and preferences.
